Plunderin'Pete

Welcome to the Plunderin'Pete project page! Plunderin'Pete is a pirate-themed adventure game where the player navigates through treacherous waters in search of hidden treasures.

Game Features

  • Exciting pirate-themed enemies and challenges

    • In Plunderin'Pete, players will encounter a variety of pirate-themed enemies, including rival pirates, sea monsters, and naval ships. Each enemy presents unique challenges that require strategic thinking and quick reflexes to overcome.

  • Harder and harder waves and enemies as you progress
  • Upgrades to your ship and weapons

    • As players progress through the game, they can earn gold and resources to upgrade their ship and weapons. Upgrades include faster sails, stronger hulls, and more powerful cannons, allowing players to take on tougher enemies and challenges.
