Troubleshoot

Welcome to the Troubleshoot project page! Troubleshoot is a top down 2D shooter game where the player fights off waves of bugs and viruses to protect their computer system.

Game Features

  • Engaging top down shooter gameplay

    • In Troubleshoot, players will navigate through various levels, using their shooting skills to eliminate waves of bugs and viruses that threaten their computer system. The game features responsive controls and dynamic enemy behavior to keep players on their toes.

  • Variety of enemies with unique behaviors
  • Power-ups and upgrades to enhance gameplay

    • As players progress through the game, they can collect power-ups and upgrades that enhance their shooting abilities and provide strategic advantages against tougher enemies.
